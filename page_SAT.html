This course is perfect for those who sits SAT II chemistry test on May 5th.   • A webinar is a LIVE stream in a closed Facebook group. You can see me (the teacher) and the blackboard - just like at a school lesson. You can also ask your questions in a chat

What is a "test practice session"?
