Privacy Policy

Google analytics

Like millions of other website owners, we use Google Analytics on UniSprint.org Google Analytics is the service that collects data about website visitors. For example, what website you came from, how long you stayed, what kind of computer you were using, and quite a bit more. No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting. You can check out Google Analytics Terms of Service for more info.

What do we do with this data?

This information helps understand the kind of people who come to UniSprint.org and what pages they are most interested in. This allows us to make better decisions about course planning. Occasionally, we compile aggregate statistics about the number of visitors this site receives and browsers being used. All of our activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service . If you want to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking,you can use an official Google opt-out add-on.

Google forms.

The registration for the online course is done via Google forms. The user himself lets us know the information such as name, age, email, country, grade, chemistry score, etc. This information would never be sold, transferred, exchanged or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased service. The email address provided in Google forms when registering for the course is used to send you course-related information, links to online webinars, and invitations to the Google class for chemistry test practice sessions. This is essential for proper participation in the course you paid for. If you do not wish to receive any of this information you can do so by replying with the topic “Unsubscribe” to any of the sent emails.

Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. Cookies are used in Google Analytics and Google Form algorithms.

Third party links

Occasionally, we may include or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

Children Online Privacy Protection

Our website and services do not specifically target people younger than 15 years old. For example, target audience of our commercial in social media is 15-18 years old. A parent should supervise actions of a child younger than 15 years old (or 13, 14, or 16 years old in some jurisdictions) on the Internet. If a child younger than 15 years old would like to participate in a course, a parent must give a permission to the child to fill in the Google forms for registration and participation in the course. If you are a parent, and your child has registered for the online course (including our free courses) without your permission, please send us an email to unisprint.amna@gmail.com, indicating your child’s email address, and his/her information will be removed, and he/she will be excluded from participation in the course.
