About Us

UniSprint is an online school where you can prepare for any chemistry test. European-quality education is now available to students from all over the world! At UniSprint, you can choose regular personal tutoring sessions or group training. Online personal training takes place via a video chat and using a virtual blackboard. Group courses consist of LIVE lectures (called webinars) followed by test practice sessions in a closed Facebook group.

How to join a group course

Choose a course

Choose a course in the "Online courses" section

Register

Pay the registration fee and indicate your email correctly

Go to the closed Facebook group

The teacher will invite you to watch LIVE lessons and take test in the closed FB group of the course

Free training

Free training goes all-year-round. You can join us any time. And guess what? All the training takes place in your favorite social networks

LIVE webinars

A webinar is like an online lesson. You watch a LIVE video where the teacher explains a chemistry topic. You can see the teacher, the "blackboard", and you can ask your questions in a chat. Check out the upcoming webinars here. Not all webinars are recorded!

Instagram

Our Instagram account has tons of engaging, easily-explained chemistry materials. Plus, we have regular free test practice sessions there (6-8 test questions per week). You can answer test questions, and in 2 days the teacher will give the correct answer and explain your mistakes if you had any.

Facebook

We also have a closed Facebook group where we post explained chemistry topics, links to our latest video lessons, and chemistry tests. You can even find full practice tests with video explanations there. In addition, you can ask any chemistry question you might have in Discussion threads.
