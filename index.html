About Us

UniSprint is an online school where you can prepare for any chemistry test. We tutor students from all over the world! The main purpose of UniSprint is to make education affordable for EVERYONE. We divided the world into 3 zones depending on the average income. So, the countries where people get lower salary will have cheaper UniSprint courses. In addition, we offer free spots at all our courses. Don't forget to check out our FREE live chemistry webinars and Instagram test practices. Welcome!

How we teach

At UniSprint, you can choose regular personal tutoring sessions or group training.

Online personal training takes place via video chat and using a virtual blackboard.

Group courses consist of LIVE lectures (called webinars) followed by test practice sessions in a closed Facebook group.

There are 3 modes of group training:

Sprint, Marathon, and IronMan.

3 modes of group training

Sprint

Duration: 2-3 weeks

Sprint is a short course on a certain chemistry topic. If you're good at self-studying, but there's this one nasty topic that you just can't get right, Sprint mode is for you.

Marathon

Duration: 1-12 months

Marathon mode is for you if you want to master test questions. Choose the exam you're taking and practice, practice, and practice.

IronMan

Duration: 10 months

This is the toughest mode. If you choose this mode you will upgrade your chemistry score from 0 to the highest. But prepare to study HARD.

Free training

Free training goes all-year-round. You can join us any time. And guess what? All the training takes place in your favorite social networks

LIVE webinars

A webinar is like an online lesson. You watch a LIVE video where the teacher explains a chemistry topic. You can see the teacher, the "blackboard", and you can ask your questions in a chat. We have FREE webinars weekly. You should try to catch it LIVE! Not all webinars are recorded!

Instagram

Our Instagram account has tons of engaging, easily-explained chemistry materials. Plus, we have regular free test practice sessions there (6-8 test questions per week). You can answer test questions, and in 2 days the teacher will give the correct answer and explain your mistakes if you had any.

Facebook

We also have a closed Facebook group where we post explained chemistry topics, links to our latest video lessons, and chemistry tests. You can even find full practice tests with video explanations there. In addition, you can ask any chemistry question you might have in Discussion threads.

Online courses

Choose one of the 4 types of chemistry training

Personal training

Personal training is a one-on-one lesson with the teacher. Just like in school. The lessons take place in a video chat where you see and talk to the teacher, just like in a Skype call. In addition, we use a shared online blackboard, where you and the teacher can write together and embed images. Perfect for solving chemistry problems! You can also choose to study with a friend in a group of 2.

Learn more

Sprint

Sprint is a short 2-week (sometimes, 3-week) course on a certain chemistry topic. If you are good at self-studying, but there's this one chemistry topic that you can't get right, then Sprint mode is for you. In Sprint courses we have 2-3 live webinars + 2 practice sessions per week. Each Sprint course has a registration deadline. Don't miss it! The course is held for a group of students.

Learn more

Marathon

Marathon is a practice course where you will master a chemistry test of your choice. Let's imagine you're taking GCSE chemistry test. Then you choose GCSE chemistry marathon. In this marathon, you will be given 1 full GCSE chemistry practice test per month that you will first solve yourself. For some exams (for example, SAT II chemistry), you'll be given 2 tests per month. Then we will have 2, or 3, LIVE webinars where the teacher will explain each test question, simple and clear. You can jump into the course any time. The course is held for a group of students.

Learn more

IronMan

IronMan course is the toughest. We'll study chemistry 6 days a week during 10 months. We will cover all the high school chemistry topics and learn how to solve problems on LIVE webinars (2 webinars/week), and have test practices (2 practices/week). You'll be given a big homework each week (2 days of work). In the end of each month, we check how much homework you have done. If it's less than 70%, you'll be expelled from the course. This course is held for a group of max 30 students. The IronMan course is not exam-specific. Join by deadline!
