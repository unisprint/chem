IronMan

When: 1 February 2018 - 1 October 2018; Price per month: 50 USD (Zone 1), 30 USD (Zone 2), 20 USD (Zone 3)

In IronMan training, you will be studying chemistry 6 days a week: 2 days of lectures, 2 days of practice sessions, 2 days for a hometask. We will cover all the topics of secondary (basic level) or high school chemistry (advanced level) over 9 months of training. IronMan course is not exam-specific, but you can choose between basic level (similar to the GCSE program) and advanced level (similar to A-level). We'll start from the very beginning. The test practice and hometasks are compulsory. If you are doing less than 70% of your tasks (checked in the end of each month), you will not be allowed to continue with the course.

Weekly schedule

  • Monday: 1-1.5 h LIVE webinar
  • Tuesday: a practice session in a closed Google class based on the topic of Monday's webinar
  • Wednesday: 1-1.5 h LIVE webinar
  • Thursday: a practice session in a closed Google class based on the topic of Wednesday's webinar
  • Friday, Saturday: hometask
  • Sunday: deadline for returning your weekly hometask

What is a webinar?

  • A webinar is a LIVE lesson on YouTube. You can see me (the teacher) and the blackboard - just like at a school lesson. You can also ask your questions in a chat
  • All the courses at UniSprint are student-friendly. I do respect your pace of studying and your schedule, so you will be provided with the link to the recorded version of the webinar in case you couldn't join us live or want to watch it again
  • You can watch the examples of my LIVE webinars on my YouTube Channel

What is "test practice in a closed Google class"?

  • After each webinar I will post problems and test questions to our closed Google class
  • A Google class looks a lot like a Facebook group. I will create posts with polls and free response questions, which you will have to solve by the end of the course
  • You can ask your questions in the comments if you have any
  • I will answer all the questions you might have in the comments or via email till the very end of the course
  • You can also have a discussion with your peer-students :)

How to register for IronMan course?

  • Fill in this application form . It is very important that write your REAL EMAIL address! Otherwise, I won't be able to contact you
  • Wait for my confirmation and invoice

Payment

  • Your IronMan course price depends on the country you live in. Check out which zone your country belongs to here. Don't forget to check whether you are eligible for a free spot at a course in the "Free course spots" section in the main menu
  • Payment is done securely via PayPal. If you don't have a PayPal account, you can register it for free here . I will pay transaction fees myself, so you don't have to pay any extra
  • The payment for the IronMan course is done once a month. The first payment has to be done no later than 24 hours before the beginning of the month
  • After I have received the payment, you will be sent a full practice test and then the links to closed webinars
  • The payments for the second and consecutive months must be done only AFTER I confirm that you are allowed to continue with the course (remember that if you do less than 70% of course practice and homework you will not be allowed to continue)

Technical issues

  • You need a Google account to ask question on a webinar and join test practice sessions in Google class. If you don't have it, register here, it's free
  • That's all you need :)

If you've missed a deadline

If you want to join after the deadline has passed, you can send your request as usually . You can jump into the course if we have free spots available. But you will need to pass a test on the topics that we have already covered with the IronMan group
