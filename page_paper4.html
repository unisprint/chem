About the course

This course is perfect to prepare for Paper 4 CIE chemistry exams. I will provide you with 3 full Paper 4. You will solve the tasks yourself first, and then you'll attend 3 live 1.5-2 hour webinars where I'll explain how to answer all these questions. You can always ask your questions in the live chat during the webinars or email me :) This is the perfect practice before your Paper 4 exam!

What is a webinar?

  • A webinar is a LIVE stream in a closed Facebook group. You can see me (the teacher) and the blackboard - just like at a school lesson. You can also ask your questions in a chat
  • All the courses at UniSprint are student-friendly. I do respect your pace of studying and your schedule, so you will be provided with the link to the recorded version of the webinar in case you couldn't join us live or want to watch it again
  • You can watch the examples of my LIVE webinars on my YouTube Channel

Technical issues

  • To attend the course all you need is a Facebook account and an internet connection. That's it.
  • You can watch live stream from a computer/tablet/phone - any devices you usually use to access your Facebook.

Payment

  • Fill in this Google form to register. Please make sure you indicate the CORRECT EMAIL ADDRESS! Otherwise, I won't be able to contact you.
  • After I have received your Google form I will send you a Paypal invoice within the next 24 hours to your EMAIL.
  • The payment is done securely via PayPal . You don't need a PayPal account to pay the fee, you only need a credit/debit card. Please note that the payment is requested in EUROS! So if you live outside the eurozone your bank or Paypal currency conversion fees may apply.
  • After I have received the payment, I will send the link to join the course Facebook group :)

Refund and cancellation policy

  • If you change your mind and decide to opt out of the course, you can get a refund.
  • You will get a 75% refund (with the subtraction of applicable Paypal and bank fees) if you cancel your participation in the course at least one week before the course starts.
  • You will get a 25% refund (with the subtraction of applicable Paypal and bank fees) if you cancel your participation 1 week to 1 day before the beginning of the course.
  • No refund is available if you cancel your participation the day before the exam and later.
