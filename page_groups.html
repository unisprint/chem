What is it all about?

You can study chemistry with me one-on-one OR in Skype-mini groups.

One-on-one lessons

One-on-one is simple: I teach you privately, by Skype, we can start the curriculum from any topic, have as many lessons as you need, the lessons will be tailored to fit your current level and needs.

Group lessons

A group consists of 3 people. The people in your group will follow the same chemistry curriculum as you do, and will be at the same chemistry knowledge level as you are. Normally, we have 2 one hour lessons per week.

What is a Skype lesson?

A Skype lesson is like a normal lesson: you will see me, the whiteboard, and your group mates if you study in mini-groups. You will be able to ask your questions during the lesson through your microphone. At the lessons, we discuss the homework, I explain new topics, and we solve problems.

How much time will it take to cover all the curriculum?

  • IGCSE chemistry - 3-4 months
  • AS-level chemistry - 4-5 months
  • A-level chemistry - 4-5 months
  • SAT chemistry - 4 months

    • Of course, this timing is approximate and depends on how fast you learn. But that's what works in 85% of cases :)
