Personal training

Duration: 60 min, Price: 30 USD

Technical issues

Personal training takes place in Google hangouts. So to be able to study you need to have:

  • A Google account. If you don't have it, register here. It's free.
  • A microphone
  • Headphones or speakers

How to start Personal training

  • Fill in this application form. It is very important that you provide your REAL email address, so that I can contact you. Also, I ask you to answer all the questions because this will help me build the lessons program that best fits your needs
  • Wait for my response. Usually, I respond within 24 hours. We will agree on lesson dates, and I will send you a free diagnostic test developed specially for you. The results of the test will help me understand your strengths and weaknesses and come up with an initial study program for you
  • I will schedule a 5-10 minutes test call a few days before our first lesson, just to make sure we don't have any technical problems
  • Please note that I don't give trial lessons. You can check out my webinars and recorded video lessons on my YouTube channel, and decide whether my teaching methods are suitable for you

About the lessons

  • The lesson starts at a certain time, please don't be late! :)
  • After each lesson you will have a compulsory homework
  • After each lesson I will send your parent/guardian a report where I will indicate the topics we've covered, how you did on your previous homework, and what your next homework is

Payment

  • The payment for the lesson is done in advance, 24 hours before the beginning of the lesson at the latest
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule a lesson, you can cancel it no later than 12 hours before the beginning
  • The payment is done securely via PayPal . You don't need a PayPal account to pay the fee, you only need a credit/debit card. I will pay transaction fees myself, so you don't have to pay any extra
  • Note that if you live in the European Union, you I will also have to include a 24% VAT (value added tax) to your invoice
